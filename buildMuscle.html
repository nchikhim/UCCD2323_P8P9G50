Weekly Workout Program

Follow our structured weekly workout program to build muscle efficiently:

Day Workout Description
Monday
Upper Body Strength Focus on arms, shoulders, and chest with weight lifting exercises.
Tuesday
Cardio 30 minutes of running or cycling to improve cardiovascular health.
Wednesday
Lower Body Strength Work on legs and glutes with exercises like squats and lunges.
Thursday
Rest or Yoga Recovery day with optional light yoga or stretching.
Friday
Full Body Workout Combine exercises from both upper and lower body routines.
Saturday
Cardio and Core Focus on cardio followed by abdominal exercises.
Sunday
Rest Complete rest to recover and prepare for the next week.
