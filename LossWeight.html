Running

Improve cardiovascular health and boost overall fitness.
Maintain an upright posture, engage your core, and land midfoot.

Swimming

Low-impact, full-body workout for all fitness levels.
Keep your body streamlined, rotate your hips, and breathe rhythmically.

Dance

Burn calories while dancing to energetic music.
Keep your core engaged, stay light on your feet, and follow the instructor's lead.

Jumping

High-intensity cardio for improved coordination and agility.
Keep your elbows close to your body, jump on the balls of your feet, and maintain a steady rhythm.
